Strategic Acquisition Timeline & Scenario Analysis

This interactive tool maps the strategic transformation of Forrestania Resources from a single-project explorer into one of Western Australia's fastest-growing gold consolidators.

Explore over 30 acquisitions across the Forrestania, Westonia, Coolgardie and Eastern Goldfields hubs, track over 700,000 ounces of JORC gold resources assembled in under 12 months, and examine the path to production at the Lake Johnston processing plant — commencing production in 2026 and targeting up to 100,000 ounces per annum by 2027.

We also analyse potential scenarios for how FRS could move to become a 300,000 ounce producer from flagged acquisition opportunities.

Navigate the timeline event-by-event or use the controls to view the full picture, compare nearby regional resources, and assess the portfolio's strategic positioning.
